About SAID

Stroke is a leading cause of death and disability worldwide, but early detection can save lives and reduce long-term complications. SAID leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence to analyze facial movements, speech patterns, and vital signs through your Android device, providing instant stroke risk assessment when every second counts.

Why SAID Exists

Every year, millions of people suffer strokes, and the golden hour for treatment is critical. SAID was developed by a team of neurologists, AI researchers, and mobile developers to democratize stroke detection technology and make it accessible to everyone.

How It Works

SAID uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze multiple indicators of stroke through your Android device

1. Scan

Use your device's camera to capture facial movements and speech patterns. The AI analyzes micro-expressions and vocal changes in real-time.

2. Detect

Advanced machine learning algorithms process the data against trained models to identify potential stroke indicators with high accuracy.

3. Alert

Receive instant feedback with risk assessment and emergency contact recommendations. Get guidance on next steps and local emergency services.
